## Why Fansly Live Downloader

Fansly is designed for streaming and on-platform access, not for keeping local copies of live sessions or saved recordings. Live broadcasts end, saved videos are not presented with a simple download workflow, and generic downloader tools often miss protected streams or struggle with member-only media.

Fansly Live Downloader is built specifically for that environment. It focuses on supported Fansly pages, detects the available media in your browser session, and gives you a direct path from stream to local MP4 file without needing desktop recording software or manual stream extraction.

## How It Works

1. Install the extension from the latest release.

## Step-by-Step Tutorial: How to Download Videos from Fansly Live

1. Install Fansly Live Downloader from the latest GitHub release.
2. Open Fansly and sign in to the account that already has access to the content.
3. Visit the live stream, saved video, or supported VR page you want to keep.
4. Let the player load fully and press play.
5. Click the extension button or the on-page control.
6. Review the quality options shown by the extension.
7. For live streams, start recording and stop it when the session is finished.
8. For saved recordings, click download and wait for the MP4 export to complete.
9. Open the finished file from your Downloads folder.

## Troubleshooting

**The extension is not detecting the stream**  
Press play first and wait a few seconds so the stream has time to initialize.

**The page control is missing**  
Open the extension popup directly. Some supported pages work better through the popup UI.

**Only one quality option is listed**  
That usually means the page is exposing a single playable stream variant.

**The recording stopped too early**  
Check whether the live session ended or your internet connection dropped during capture.

**The page requires subscription or paid access**  
The extension only works on media you can already open and play in your active browser session.
